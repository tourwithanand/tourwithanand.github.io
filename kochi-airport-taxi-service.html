How our service works

A clear, simple process to book and receive your transfer with minimal effort.

1. Send details on WhatsApp

Use the form to prefill a message, or message us directly on WhatsApp. Provide flight number, arrival time and pickup details.

2. We confirm and track

Our team confirms your booking and we track your flight. Drivers wait at arrivals and adjust for delays.

3. Meet & greet

Driver meets you at the arrival gate with a nameboard. If you have special requests (child seat, extra luggage), let us know.

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I book?

A: Use the contact form above or message on WhatsApp. Provide your name, phone, flight number and pickup details. We will confirm availability quickly.

Q: Do you accept corporate bookings?

A: Yes. We provide corporate invoicing and monthly billing for registered companies — contact us on WhatsApp to register your account.

Q: Are tolls and parking included?

A: Tolls and parking are billed extra as applicable. We communicate any route-specific charges before the trip on request.

Q: What if my flight is delayed?

A: We track flights and adjust pickup time accordingly. If there is an extended delay, we will contact you and the driver will wait at the adjusted time.

Q: Can I request a baby seat or extra luggage?

A: Yes — please add these requests in the message at booking time and we will arrange accordingly (subject to vehicle availability).

Q: Do you offer long-distance transfers outside Kerala?

A: Yes. Please message your itinerary on WhatsApp and we will propose a vehicle and driver for long-distance journeys.

Terms & Policies

Please read these terms carefully before booking. These terms explain our refund, cancellation and waiting policies.

Cancellation policy

Cancellations must be communicated on WhatsApp. For short-notice cancellations (within 2 hours of pickup), a partial cancellation fee may apply depending on driver allocation and waiting time.

Waiting time

Drivers wait for the grace period agreed at the time of booking. Extended waiting due to flight delays is handled case-by-case; we strive to be fair in charging for driver time if requested by the driver.

Liability

We operate as a transport service provider. While we ensure drivers are licensed and vehicles are insured, passengers are responsible for personal items. Report any issues via WhatsApp within 24 hours so we can assist.

Privacy

We collect minimal information (name, phone, pickup details) to confirm bookings. We do not share personal data with third parties except as required to provide the service (e.g., with drivers).

Travel tips for Kochi visitors
