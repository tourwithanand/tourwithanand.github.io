Meet Anand - Your South India Tour Guide

Today, our experienced team operates a fleet of well-maintained vehicles from sedans to Tempo Travellers, serving families, NRI visitors, pilgrims, and corporate groups. Our drivers are trained in local languages and know the safest, most scenic routes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Kochi airport taxi and South India tour services

Do you provide 24/7 Kochi airport pickup service? +
Yes, we provide round-the-clock Kochi International Airport (COK) pickup service. Our drivers track your flight status and wait at the arrivals area with a name board, regardless of delays or early arrivals.
Which South India destinations do you cover in your tour packages? +
We cover all major South India destinations including Kerala (Munnar, Alleppey, Thekkady, Wayanad), Tamil Nadu (Madurai, Rameswaram, Kanyakumari, Ooty, Kodaikanal), and Karnataka (Mysore, Coorg, Bangalore). Custom multi-state circuits are our specialty.
What types of vehicles are available for South India tours? +
Our fleet includes sedans (3-4 passengers), Ertiga (6 passengers), Innova Crysta (7 passengers), and Tempo Travellers (12-20 passengers). All vehicles are AC, well-maintained, and suitable for long-distance South India touring with comfortable seating and luggage space.
How is the pricing calculated for South India taxi packages? +
Pricing is based on per-day rates depending on vehicle type and tour duration. We provide transparent quotes including toll charges, parking fees, and driver allowances. No hidden costs - you pay 50% advance and balance at trip completion.
Can South India tour itineraries be customized? +
Absolutely! All our South India tours are fully customizable. Whether you prefer more temples, hill stations, beaches, wildlife, or cultural experiences, we create personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and time availability.
Do you help with hotel bookings during South India tours? +
Yes, we can suggest and help book accommodations ranging from budget hotels to luxury resorts based on your preferences. We have partnerships with reliable properties across South India and can arrange complete packages or just transportation as per your needs.
Are your services suitable for elderly travelers and families with children? +
Yes, we specialize in family-friendly and elderly-comfortable tours. Child seats are available on request, and we can adjust itineraries for slower-paced travel with more rest stops. Our drivers are experienced in assisting elderly passengers and families with special needs.
What is your cancellation and refund policy? +
Our cancellation policy is clearly communicated before booking confirmation. Generally, cancellations made 7+ days before travel receive full refund minus processing charges. We understand travel plans can change and try to be as flexible as possible with our valued guests.
