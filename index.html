Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I meet my driver at Kochi Airport?

Your driver will be waiting at the designated visitor meeting area just outside the Arrivals Terminal (T1 for Domestic or T3 for International) holding a placard with your name.

Are there any night charges?

We do not have hidden night surcharges. The price quoted to you is the final price, regardless of your arrival time. We operate 24/7.

What if my flight is delayed?

We monitor flight schedules using your flight number. If your flight is delayed, our driver will adjust the pickup time automatically at no extra cost.

How do I pay?

You can pay via Cash or UPI (GPay/PhonePe) directly to the driver after reaching your destination. For some bookings, a small token advance may be required.
