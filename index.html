Must-Visit Places & Activities

UNESCO Sites

01
Tamilnadu

MAHABALIPURAM

Mahabalipuram, a 7th-century Pallava port city, is known for the Shore Temple, Pancha Rathas, and rock-cut caves.

02
Karnataka

HAMPI

The 14th-century Vijayanagara capital, is famed for its grand temples, stone architecture, and historic landscape.

03
Karnataka

PATTADAKAL

Pattadakkal, from the 7th–8th century, blends Dravidian and Nagara styles with intricately carved temples.

04
Tamil Nadu

BRIHADESHWARA TEMPLE

Built by Raja Raja Chola I in the 11th century, Brihadeeswara Temple showcases Chola grandeur.

05
Karnataka

CHENNAKESAVA TEMPLE

Chennakeshava Temple in Hampi, built by the Vijayanagara rulers, features intricate carvings.
