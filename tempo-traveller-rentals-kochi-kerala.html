Frequently Asked Questions

What is a tempo traveller and how many people can it carry?

A tempo traveller is a comfortable, medium-sized van/mini-bus used for group travel. Our fleet ranges from 9–12, 13–16 and 18–25 seaters to suit different group sizes.

Do you provide airport pickup at Kochi (COK) 24/7?

Yes — we provide 24/7 airport pickups and drops. Please share your flight details and contact number when booking.

How are fares calculated?

Fares depend on vehicle type, distance, and whether the booking is a single transfer or multi-day hire. We provide transparent, itemized quotes including tolls and driver allowance where applicable.

Can we request stops for sightseeing?

Yes — flexible stops for sightseeing, meals or restroom breaks can be included. For long sightseeing days, consider a multi-day hire for best value.

Is luggage space sufficient for large groups?

Tempo travellers have dedicated luggage space. For large amounts of luggage, we recommend informing us in advance so we can suggest the suitable vehicle size.

How do I confirm a booking?

Confirm via WhatsApp or phone after we send the quote. We’ll hold the vehicle and send driver details and a confirmation message.
